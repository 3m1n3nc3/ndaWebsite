BIOGRAPHY

Brigadier General Ibrahim Mohammed Jallo was commissioned Second Lieutenant on 25 September 1993 and posted into the Nigerian Army Armoured Corps. He has attended several military courses among which are Young Officers’ Course Infantry, Young Officers’ Course Armour, Anti Tank Platoon Commanders, Mortar Platoon Commander, Basic and Advance Range Management. Others are Armour Company, Battalion Commanders, Junior and Senior Course at Armed Forces Command and Staff College and National Defence College at Abuja, Nigeria.

Brigadier General Jallo was a Platoon and Company Commander in 244 Recce Battalion, Saki Oyo State, Commanding Officer of 221 Battalion, Kainji as well as 245 Recce Battalion in Ikom Cross River State. He took part in Operation FLUSHOUT II in Damaturu, Yobe State, Military Observer in African Mission in Sudan and also served as an Instructor in Nigerian Defence Academy from 2002 – 2004 and 2011 (Senior Instructor). Brigadier General Jallo was also Directing Staff and Chief Instructor in Armed Forces Command and Staff College, and Chief Instructor in Nigerian Army Peace Keeping Corps, Staff Officer Grade 2 and Staff Officer Grade one Force Generation at Directorate of Peacekeeping in Army Headquarters, Military Assistant of Chief of Training and Operations (Army), Force Commander in Darfur and Deputy Chief of Staff Training and Operation at Army Headquarters Garrison, Abuja.

Prior to his posting to the Nigerian Defence Academy as Registrar, he was the Chief of Staff in 3 Div Jos. His academic qualifications include Bachelor of Science (Honours) Chemistry, Postgraduate Diploma in Management from ABU Zaria; Masters in International Affairs and Strategic Studies, Nigerian Defence Academy; Master of Science in Strategic Studies from University of Ibadan and currently undergoing a Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) programme in Nasarawa State University, Keffi. He is also an Associate Member of National Institute of Management. Brigadier General Jallo is married and blessed with 5 children. He enjoys reading and jogging.
