BIOGRAPHY

Major General Adeniyi Oyebade is a member of 32 Regular Course and was commissioned into the Nigerian Army Infantry Corps on 22 June 1985. His academic qualifications include Bachelor of Sciences and Masters of Sciences degrees from University of Baluchistan, Pakistan and University of Ibadan, Nigeria respectively. He has attended several professional courses including Junior Staff Courses at the Armed Forces Command and Staff College (AFCSC), Nigeria and Army Senior Staff Course in Pakistan. He was a participant at the National Defence College (NDC), Course 18 where he bagged the Chief of Army Staff Merit Award for the best research paper on Land Operations. In his career, Major General Oyebade has held instructional, staff and command appointments and also has a rich operational experience. He was in ECOMOG Operation as OC Alpha Company, NIBATT 4, Liberia in 1991 and Operation HARMONY IV with 131 Special Task Force Battalion, Bakassi in 1994 He was OC Bravo Company NIBATT 23 ECOMOG Liberia/Sierra Leone from 1995-1998. He later became Sector Commander, Operation HAKURI, Escravos in the Niger Delta in 1999 Major General Oyebade was a Directing Staff both at AFCSC and NDC. He was equally the Commander at 82 Division Garrison at different times between 2008 and 2009. He was commander 9 Brigade Nigerian Army from February till December 2013 after which he was appointed Deputy Commandant/Director of Studies at the NDC, an appointment he held until 17 August 2015. He was the GOC I Division Nigerian Army from 17 August 2015 till 26 October 2017 when he took over as the 28th Commandant of the Nigerian Defence Academy. Major General Oyebade is happily married to Mrs Edeoghene Oghize Oyebade and he is a proud father and grandfather. At his leisure, he enjoys playing golf, swimming and reading autobiographies on strategie leadership.
